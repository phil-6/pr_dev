Transparency, Accountablity, Collaboration

Why I'm standing as a trustee for WBMRT

Tl;dr: I'm Phil, I'm standing as a trustee as I want to try to increase the transparency, accountability and collaboration of the team's management.

Hi All,

For those who don't know me; Hi! I'm Phil! My day job is the Lead Software Developer at the Legal Innovation Lab Wales, but I also spend a reasonable amount of time working as an outdoor guide, primarily leading groups gorge walking in the waterfalls area and coasteering at Rhossili.

I have a few useful qualifications including Rescue3 WRT-Pro, I'm also working towards my RCI and ML. This outdoor experience and my day job have given me a wealth of leadership experience.

I joined the team 2 years ago because I wanted to use my skills and experience to help people who needed it most.

This has been an eventful year within the team. It's never an ideal time when trustees and team officers resign and EGMs are called. I am not naive enough to think that I can fix everything, but I think I can help, and more than anything, I want to try.

If I am elected as a trustee I will aim to increase transparency, accountability and collaboration.

We are a team of volunteers with the shared goal of helping people in need. I feel as though we should share all information openly within the team, by increasing transparency we can increase accountability to each other. I think that all information should be easily discoverable by those who want to access it. While some personal information will obviously need to remain confidential, I do not feel as though we gain anything by siloing information.

As a trustee, I will continue to endeavor to hold true to the principles I follow in life. I will be honest, kind and open. I will take ownership of my actions and decisions and aim to leverage the wealth of experience and skills around me to improve things for everyone.

If you would like to chat please feel free to reach out via email, phone or message.

Thank you for taking the time to read this,
Best Wishes,
