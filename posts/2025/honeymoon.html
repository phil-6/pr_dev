Honeymoon

Shona and I had the trip of a lifetime to New Zealand for our honeymoon. We spent 3 and a bit weeks travelling around the North and South Islands, visiting some of the most incredible places in the world.

We started in Auckland, then headed down to Waitomo via Hamilton and Bridal Veil Falls. After Waitomo, we had a couple of days in Taupo. Next we did the Tongariro Alpine Crossing, before heading to Wellington and the South Island. On the South Island we did a three-day walk in the Abel Tasman National Park. After all that walking we had a couple of days at a resort in the Milford Sounds. We then procceded down South Island via Christchurch and Mount Cook. A couple of days in Wanaka before finishing our trip in Queenstown.

Full Itinerary
  • Day 1 - 15th March: Arrive in Auckland. Stay 1: Airedale Boutique Suites
  • Day 2 - 16th March: Drive to Waitomo via Hamilton and Bridal Veil Falls. Stay 2: Waitomo Village Chalets
  • Day 3 - 17th March: Waitomo Caves and drive to Taupo. Stay 3: Beachtree Suites
  • Day 4 - 18th March: Walk from hotel to the Taupo Spa Thermal Park and Huka Falls Stay 4: Beachtree Suites
  • Day 5 - 19th March: Wai-o-taupo Thermal Wonderland and Waikite Valley Hot Pools. Stay 5: The Park Hotel Ruapehu
  • Day 6 - 20th March: Tongariro Alpine Crossing. Stay 6: The Park Hotel Ruapehu
  • Day 7 - 21st March: Drive to Wellington. Stay 7: The Rydges Wellington
  • Day 8 - 22nd March: Ferry to Picton and drive to Motueka. Stopped at Pelorus Bridge for a swim Stay 8: The Motueka Garden Motel
  • Day 9 - 23rd March: Abel Tasman National Park. Water taxi from Motueka to Totaranui then walk to Awaroa. Stay 9: Meadow Homestead At Awaroa
  • Day 10 - 24th March: Walk from Awaroa to Torrent Bay Stay 10: Torrent Bay Lodge
  • Day 11 - 25th March: Walk from Torrent Bay to Marahau. Water taxi back to Motueka. Stay 11: Parautane Lodge – Nelson
  • Day 12 - 26th March: Drive to Picton. Water taxi Punga Cove. Stay 12: Punga Cove Resort
  • Day 13 - 27th March: Chill at Punga Cove, short kayak on the Sound. Stay 13: Punga Cove Resort
  • Day 14 - 28th March: Water taxi back to Picton. Drive to Christchurch. Stay 14: Peppers Clearwater Resort
  • Day 15 - 29th March: Christchurch explore, Quake City museum. Drive to Mount Cook. Stay 15: Mount Cook Lodge & Motels
  • Day 16 - 30th March: Walk the Hooker Valley Trail. Stay 16: Mount Cook Lodge & Motels
  • Day 17 - 31st March: Tasman Glacier View and drive to Wanaka Stay 17: Edgewater Wanaka
  • Day 18 - 1st April: WildWire Wanaka - Via Ferrata Stay 18: Edgewater Wanaka
  • Day 19 - 2nd April: Drive to Queenstown via Arrowtown. Onsen Hot Pools. Stay 19: QT Queenstown
  • Day 20 - 3rd April: Milford Sound Stay 20: QT Queenstown
  • Day 21 - 4th April: Cancelled Dart River Adventure, so explored Queenstown Stay 21: QT Queenstown
  • Day 22 - 5th April: Dart River Adventures - Funyaks Safari Stay 22: QT Queenstown
  • Day 23 - 6th April: Begin the Journey home
